Whales

By: Vi (5/6J)

Whales are the biggest animals in the world. They live in the ocean and they are mammals, which means they have babies and breathe air.

There are different kinds of whales. My favourite is the blue whale.

Whales talk to each other with songs and sounds. Some people say it's like music.

We need to protect whales from pollution.

I hope to see a real whale one day!
